Registration Guidelines

Welcome to the First Philippine Esperanto Youth Congress! We would like to thank you for your interest in this event. Before proceeding with the registration, please read the following guidelines.

Who can join?

Participation is open to any person of any age, Philippine or foreign national, who has at least a basic command of Esperanto and is interested to learn more of the language and its community and culture, participate in the activities of the congress, and actively interact with other participants.

Registration Fee

The registration fee for the congress depends on three main factors: the individual's age at the time of registration, the individual's country of origin, and the period in which registration and payment are made. Individuals who register early can avail of discounted fees. There is also a separate category for individuals who wish to financially support the Congress, and separate fees for foreign participants.

The fee includes the participant's kit and access to all programs (unless otherwise indicated in the congress booklet). It does not include lodging, other meals, the excursion, and insurances.

The registration fee is strictly non-transferable and non-refundable, regardless of whether the participant fails to attend the Congress through their own volition or due to any other cause, or if the Congress is cancelled or postponed for any reason.
