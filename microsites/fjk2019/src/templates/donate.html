We need your help!

The Philippine Esperanto Youth (FEJ) is pleased to announce, that for the first time in the history of the movement of speakers, learners, and advocates of the international language Esperanto in the country, the 1st Philippine Esperanto Youth Congress will be held in Manila on April 23-26, 2020.

To guarantee the success of this congress, the Congress Organizing Committee are requesting everyone to support this initiative financially or through material support. Through your donation, you will help give Filipino Esperanto speakers and learners the opportunity to experience a truly multicultural atmosphere, consolidate the Esperanto movement in the Philippines, and ensure the continuous growth of the local community.

The Congress Fund

This fund will be used to finance the whole congress, including venue rentals, accommodation, and others. A proposed budget plan will be released immediately once the LKK has approved it.

Eventually, your contributions to the congress fund will generally

  • finance and implement important parts of the event
  • keep the registration fees low enough for young participants
  • fund the succeeding activities of Philippine Esperanto Youth, in case of a profit

Additionally, in case that we are able to collect enough, we will use part of the funds to finance participants from distant regions, who may have difficulties in joining due to economic reasons.
